Pudding

This sweet rice pudding is thick, creamy, and utterly decadent.
Made with basic ingredients you likely already have on hand,
this rice pudding recipe is the perfect comforting treat.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Egg
  • Vanilla
  • Raisins

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Pour water into a saucepan and bring to a boil over medium heat; stir in rice.
    Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender
    and liquid has been absorbed, about 20 minutes.
  3. Combine cooked rice, 1 Â½ cups milk, sugar, and salt in a clean saucepan.
    Cook over medium heat, stirring often, until thick and creamy, about 15 minutes.
  4. Stir in remaining 1/2 cup milk, beaten egg, and raisins;
    cook 2 minutes more, stirring constantly. Remove from heat
    and stir in butter and vanilla until combined..
  5. Serve warm.
